What has Auckland got for you?

The City of Auckland, New Zealand's largest city with a population of around 1.8 million people, is one of the most liveable cities in the world.

Economist Intelligence Unit's(EIU) rated it as one of the most liveable cities in 2021.

Auckland is well known for its beautiful harbour, amazing beaches, forest and great places to eat and relax and places for you to explore as well.

As well as a great night life. Auckland is an excellence city for mandy academic, sporting and leisure activites.

It is within easy access to many of New Zealand's other main cities and attractions.

Auckland has a great climate as well ~ not too hold, not too cold ~ usually just right, averaging 24 degrees in summer and about 15 degrees in winter

Having weather like this, means you can take advantage of all that there is to see and do around the city.
